What is Red Dot Gaming?

Red Dot Gaming is a South-East Asian based Gaming community. We're friendly,
Welcoming and most Important of all, Premium! We run our own servers,
from the best providers out there. With the best network & hardware.

Who runs Red Dot Gaming?

Run by the previous owners of Ragetears, Asterim Gaming & RthaurG. We are ambitious,
dedicated to always improve, update and launch renowned game servers.

Who are we?

We're Gamers, Developers, Web Designers, Students and any other jobs you can think of!
We're fellow gamers, and we strive to ever provide good game-servers.
